  • Practical lessons for line cooks!

  • Culinary school teaches the fundamentals of cooking technique, but in all reality it will never be able to prepare you for what awaits on your first day. A person could know a million recipes inside-out, and still crash and burn on any station in a restaurant.

  • Cookstart provides a series of lessons that if applied will take your abilities to another level. Better cooks earn better pay, have more job opportunities, and are highly valued. Give cookstart a shot today!

Cookstart Lessons:

  • lesson 1

    Prep List 101

    A prep-list is more than a list of items that you need for service. when done properly, it's the ultimate guide, keeping you on track, and ensuring that no minute of your preparation goes to waste!

  • lesson 2

    Taste Everything

    A great pallette takes years to develop, and no one ever really explains how its done. Learning how to taste helps develop a library of flavors that can be essential in understanding how to create your own dishes.

  • lesson 3

    Mise en Place

    A good chef can take one look at your station and tell a lot about what kind of cook you are. Mise en place is the cornerstone of organization. How you work is everybit as important as what youre working on.
